PBB Celebrity Collab
Edition

Pinoy Big Brother: Celebrity Collab Edition is the third celebrity season and the eighteenth overall season of the Philippine reality show Pinoy Big Brother. It premiered on March 9, 2025, on GMA Network. It is the first season to air on GMA and the first full celebrity edition season to air since 2008. Furthermore, this is the first season of Pinoy Big Brother to not air on ABS-CBN's domestic television channels: Kapamilya Channel and A2Z. The season features celebrities signed to the talent agencies Star Magic and Sparkle GMA Artist Center as housemates.Creative Commons license.
