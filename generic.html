PBB Celebrity
Collab Edition

Another historic collaboration between ABS-CBN and GMA

PBB is a popular reality TV show in the Philippines based on the international Big Brother franchise. It first aired on ABS-CBN in 2005 and quickly became one of the country’s most-watched reality programs. The concept of the show is simple: a group of strangers, called “housemates,” live together in a house equipped with cameras that capture their every move. They must complete tasks and face various challenges while avoiding eviction by their fellow housemates and the public. Each season has a different theme, and the show often features a mix of personalities from different walks of life, which leads to dramatic moments, emotional breakthroughs, and major twists. The person who lasts the longest, earning the most votes and completing the tasks, wins a cash prize and is crowned “Big Winner.”

Among the PBB winners through the years are Nene Tamayo (2005), Keanna Reeves (2006), Kim Chiu (2006, Teen Edition), Ejay Falcon (Teen Edition Plus), Melai Cantiveros (Double Up), James Reid (Teen Clash 2010), Slater Young (Unlimited), Myrtle Sarrosa (Teen Edition 4), Daniel Matsunaga (All-In), and Maymay Entrata (Lucky Season 7).
